Queenstown is nestled in the heart of the Southern Alps, boasting magnificent mountains for skiers and snowboarders of all levels. There are four ski areas near Queenstown: Coronet Peak being the closest (just 25 minutes from town centre) and Treble Cone being the farthest (1 hour drive). Our favourite, so far, though is The Remarkables (35-45 minutes from town), with their Ice Bar on top of the mountain making it a unique place to have a hot cup of chocolate or a glass of champagne.
