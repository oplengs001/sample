Flights from Manila

There are no direct flights from Manila to Queenstown, however Philippine Airlines has non-stop flights to Auckland, New Zealand that flies three times a week and leaves at night and lands at 13:00 the following day. Total flight time from Manila to Auckland is about 9h45m.

www.philippineairlines.com

A separate domestic flight via Air New Zealand will then have to be taken to Queenstown that departs at 17:20 and arrives Queenstown at 19:15 (please do check Air New Zealand’s website for any changes to their flight times). Total flight time from Auckland to Queenstown is 1h55m. www.airnewzealand.co.nz/
Alternatively, Qantas and Singapore Airlines also flies to Queenstown via other Asian countries.

Flights from the US and Canada

Air New Zealand is the best airline to take if you’re travelling from North America.

Los Angles (LAX), San Francisco (SFO) and Vancouver (YVR) are well connected to Queenstown via Air New Zealand with a stopover in Auckland. Total flight time is about 16-17h

www.airnewzealand.co.nz/
