Good news! Queenstown is a very small town and it only takes 15 minutes to get from the airport to the centre of Queenstown where all the hotels, restaurants and shops can be found.

Although taxis and ubers are readily available, if you plan on visiting ski resorts or wineries we highly recommend renting a vehicle for the duration of your stay. There are multiple car hire facilities in Queenstown and we do recommend AVIS Queenstown or Hertz Queenstown

Tip: book a 4WD vehicle and make sure the vehicle comes with snow chains if you plan on going up to the ski resorts as roads can become icy or slippery due to snowfall.

For public transportation from the airport:

Taxis: It takes around NZD$40-50 to get a taxi from the airport to the centre of Queenstown with Blue Bubble Taxis or Green Cabs.

Buses: Ritchies Connectabus has got busses leaving every 20 minutes from the airport to the centre, costing NZD$12. Take Connectabus 11/12 to head straight to the centre of town. If you are planning to travel by bus more often, buy a GoCard at the airport.

Getting Around Queenstown

The centre of Queenstown is small and charming, making it easy and to get around on foot! Queenstown encourages people to walk, bike or cycle around town with perfect lakeside roads dedicated to pedestrians and cyclists.
