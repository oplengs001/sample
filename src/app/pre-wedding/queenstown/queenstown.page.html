We chose Queenstown as our wedding destination for a number of reasons. Besides being a quick flight from Australia and Tommy’s family, Queenstown is such a picturesque city with its lakeside position surrounded by snow-capped mountains, making it look post card ready from every angle. Being the adventure capital of world (fun fact: Queenstown is the birthplace of bungee jumping) also meant that we could keep ourselves busy with our favorite endorphin inducing activity: snowboarding! Then, of course, there’s the amazing Pinot Noirs of Central Otago, the local cheese and the talented chefs. It just had everything that we would normally look for in a vacation: nature-driven activity, beautiful scenery, amazing food and wine! And what better excuse than a wedding to get all our loved ones to experience a place that we absolutely adore! As you can see, we love trading in skyscrapers for the great outdoors and we are absolutely thrilled that you will be coming along this great adventure with us.
