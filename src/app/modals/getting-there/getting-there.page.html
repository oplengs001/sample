Get Me There Close Jack's Retreat Map and Direction
See on Google Maps
Directions are as follows & shown on map attached:
Drive from Frankton, along State Highway 6 and enter into Jacks Point.
Drive down Maori Jack Road towards the Clubhouse.
Drive past the road into the Clubhouse, and follow the road around past tennis courts & playground on your left, and then turn right into Lodge Rd, drive through the gates and head up the hill (Ignore the 'Private no cars allowed' sign).
At top of hill turn right into Preserve Drive.
Follow Preserve Drive road around, take the 3rd road on the left into Hidden Island Rd, then right into Back Shed Rd.
Venue/house is at end on the right. Turn into courtyard at stone wall on right, just past a large tussock covered mound.
